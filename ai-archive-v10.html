A graphic designer and art director with over a decade of practice at the intersection of brand, image, and language. Based in Amsterdam, working internationally.

The work spans visual identity, editorial art direction, campaign imagery, and moving image — often in the same breath. Clients have included cultural institutions, fashion houses, independent publishers, and a handful of companies worth admiring.

Drawn to work that has a point of view. Wary of work that explains itself too much. Interested in the space between a thing and its image — what gets compressed, what gets lost, what survives.

Available for commissions, collaborations, and conversations worth having.
